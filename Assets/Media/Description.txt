Through the power of property investments, you've decided to take on the challenge to build a massive portfolio of property.

Collect rent each week and use the money wisely to take on the Real Estate world. Start with a property or from the bare minimum and climb the ladder!

Buy up to 12 houses and earn your way to the largest Real Estate Empire this world has seen!

Features:
     - Prestige system to boost your real estate adventure!
     - Achievements to help track your progress!
     - Automation to progress with ease!
     - Card style gameplay - Pick and Choose your houses wisely from a Deck!
